# Quick Task: Fix team member count displaying 0 on participant QR page and enable team leader scanning

This quick task addresses the issue where teams with no guest members display "0 members" on their team pass QR page. It also ensures that the team leader is registered as a scannable member when the team registration is completed so that organizers can scan and log attendance or other events for the team leader.

## Verification Criteria
- [ ] Django backend system check and tests pass.
- [ ] Overridden `save()` on `Team` model automatically creates a `TeamMember` record for the leader when `is_registered` is set to `True`.
- [ ] Overridden `save()` on `Team` model removes the leader's `TeamMember` record if `is_registered` becomes `False`.
- [ ] The member count on the Team Pass page displays the correct total count (including the leader).
- [ ] The Team Pass page and Hackathon Hub do not display the leader twice (once under Leader and once under Guest Teammates).
- [ ] The organizer's QR scanner checklist includes the team leader as a scannable participant.

## must_haves
- Override `Team.save()` in `backend/participant/models.py` to manage `TeamMember` for the leader.
- Update `ParticipantTeamPassView` and `ParticipantHackathonHubView` in `backend/participant/views.py` to exclude the leader's email from guest `members` queryset.
- Update the count rendering in `frontend/templates/participant/team_pass.html` to be `members.count|add:"1"`.
- Update the QR scanner test suite to verify the leader is returned in scanning checklists.
